Why attent?


This drama is a complex and powerful Hip-Hop performance of Romeo and Juliet.
With music, dance and VR it is very immersive experience that inspires.
This production is realised by a young creative team led by experienced director John Murphy and promises a fresh new take on a classic.

Ireland has one of the highest teenage suicide rates in EU.

Romeo and Juliet is a classical play that ends with a teen suicide and we want to raise awareness for this topic.
Through our performance we want to show the audience the importance of unity and helping each other.
We collaborated with different community-based organisations,
HSE and National Office for Suicide Prevention while aproaching this topic.


Romeo and Juliet is an innovative storytelling experience using live actors in VR.

As we put on VR goggles we enter a new world,
World full of colors, music, dancers…
During this performance, while diving in immersive experience we hear narrator quoting original verses,
linking this new take with a classic Romeo and Juliet play.


Romeo and Juliet in this performance are teens from this day and age.

They love hip hop, they are great dancers, they are kids that you can see at street, school…
Hip hop is music that bring energy and easily describes emotions and struggles
Through dance and music our performers make this classical story much more reliable
and teen suicide prevention message closer to people that need to her it.
